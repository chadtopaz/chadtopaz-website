Rebuttinator
Welcome to the Rebuttinator

This free tool lets you create polished, professional annotated rebuttals and critical analyses—no coding required. Here’s how it works:

  1. Fill in some basic document info and customize your annotation categories (or keep the defaults).
  2. Paste the full text you want to annotate.
  3. Click and drag to select passages, then rate and comment on each one.
  4. Preview your finished document and download it as a self-contained HTML file you can open in any browser—or print to PDF.

You can save your work-in-progress at any time (as a JSON file) and load it later to pick up where you left off. Use the buttons in the dark bar above. Want to see what the finished product looks like? View an example annotated document.

Support this project & social justice: The Rebuttinator is free and was developed without institutional resources. Privacy: The Rebuttinator runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server. Your text and annotations never leave your computer.
